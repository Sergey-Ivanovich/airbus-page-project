AIRBUS A320

The world's two leading narrowbody jets are variations of the Boeing 737 and the Airbus A320. They might not be as glamorous as their larger and more imposing siblings, such as the 787 or the A350, but they are trusted workhorses of most fleets all over the world.

AIRBUS A350

The A350 is truly innovative, from the wing design that morphs and changes shape in flight for optimal efficiency to the Airbus-pioneered fly-by-wire flight controls and onboard systems for reduced pilot workload.
